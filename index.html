Accessible Tiles

Interactive tiles are a common design pattern found on the web. With care, we can make them fancy & fun while still working with screen readers. Here are two approaches I've tested with Safari & VoiceOver.

Version 1

Tiles are interactive but the front & back of each tile always reads aloud to screen readers regardless of visual state.

Version 2

The wrapping element toggles the front & back via click & enter key. Tiles also have internal links for screen readers to toggle visibility.
